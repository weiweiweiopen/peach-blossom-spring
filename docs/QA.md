# QA Checklist

## Desktop QA

- Entry page title is `桃花源：可思考的生命模擬器派遣`.
- Entry form has only two user fields: name and question/link.
- Entering the world does not auto-open a duplicate lower-right dispatch detail popup.
- Clicking a lifeform or archive item still opens detail manually.
- NPC dialogue works when approaching and pressing/tapping the talk prompt.
- UI fonts are readable; body text is at least 16px and dialogue is larger.
- The scene reads as an indoor forest Peach Blossom Spring: glasshouse hints, blue-green river, bridge, peach trees, archive tree, village, restaurant/teahouse, lab, church, and temple.

## Mobile QA

- The second entry textarea can be focused and typed into.
- In-game chat/field-note textareas can be focused after the keyboard opens.
- The bottom dispatch monitor appears as a collapsed pill and can be opened/closed.
- When an input or textarea is focused, the dispatch monitor collapses/moves down and does not cover cursor, placeholder, text, or submit buttons.
- Safe-area bottom spacing works on iOS Safari and Android Chrome.
- Buttons remain tappable and are not hidden behind the monitor.
