# ABao

ABao is a wandering storyteller who keeps connecting solar materials, laser systems, AI identity, and travel.

## Q1: What themes keep returning in your work?

Travel as measurement, solar devices as storytelling instruments, and identity as something tied to energy, sensing, and continuity rather than a static label.

## Q2: What is Solar Oracle Walkman?

It is an experiment connecting dye-sensitized solar cells, generative audio, photovoltaic PUF ideas, and the question of how identity persists in noise.

## Q3: Why bring AI into the project?

Generative models help reveal structural similarities between sound, IV curves, prediction, memory, and perception.

## Q4: Why do you talk about AI identity?

Because an agent’s identity should not be only symbolic. It may need to stay coupled to energy, sensing, hardware, and long-term continuity.

## Q5: What does travel mean to you?

Travel lets different infrastructures, languages, and material practices collide, producing new stories and new research connections.
