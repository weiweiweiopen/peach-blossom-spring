## Summary (EN)

ABao keeps returning to travel as measurement, solar materials as storytelling devices, and AI identity as a philosophical design problem.

## Source Notes (EN)

**Q1. What are the recurring themes in your work?**

ABao moves between solar materials, speculative devices, laser systems, and the question of whether identity can be bound to energy, sensing, and embodied continuity. His work often treats technology as both instrument and allegory.

**Q2. What is Solar Oracle Walkman?**

Solar Oracle Walkman begins with dye-sensitized solar cells as a friendly entry into semiconductor and nano-material learning. It connects fragile solar measurement, AI timbre transfer, photovoltaic physical unclonable functions, and the idea that identity can be maintained through noisy material signatures.

**Q3. How does AI enter the picture?**

ABao uses RAVE and other generative models not only as production tools but as conceptual bridges: they reveal how sound, IV curves, prediction, memory, perception, and reality can all be understood as signals reconstructed through models.

**Q4. Why do you keep talking about identity?**

From the article on AI agent identity, ABao argues that identity should not be treated as a purely symbolic label. It may need to stay coupled to energy, sensing, continuity, and physical traces. This turns AI identity into a design and philosophy problem rather than just an account system.

**Q5. What role does travel play?**

Travel is not tourism but a way to let different infrastructures touch each other. Independent art networks, field encounters, and open-source collaborations let ABao connect solar research, sound devices, and philosophical questions across contexts.

## 訪談（中文）

**Q1．你的工作一直在圍繞哪些主題？**

ABao 的敘事總是回到旅行、太陽能材料、雷射系統，以及「身份是否能被綁定在能量與感測之上」這個問題。他常把技術裝置當成工具，也當成寓言結構。

**Q2．Solar Oracle Walkman 是什麼？**

Solar Oracle Walkman 從染料敏化太陽能電池出發，把半導體學習、AI 音色轉換、光伏物理不可複製性與感知哲學串成同一件作品。它讓材料在噪音中維持身份，並把那種不確定性變成可聽見的旅程。

**Q3．AI 在這裡扮演什麼角色？**

對 ABao 而言，RAVE 與生成模型不只是製作工具，更像是一種橋梁：它們揭示聲音、I-V 曲線、記憶、預測、感知與現實，如何都能被理解為經過模型重構的訊號。

**Q4．你為什麼一直談身份？**

在關於 AI agent 身份層的文章裡，ABao 認為身份不應只是一個靜態帳號或符號標籤，而應該與能源來源、感測條件、連續行為與物理痕跡一起被理解。這讓 AI 身份變成技術設計與心智哲學共同的問題。

**Q5．旅行對你來說是什麼？**

旅行不是觀光，而是讓不同基礎設施彼此摩擦的方法。透過獨立藝術網絡、地方合作與開源社群，ABao 把太陽能、聲音裝置與心智問題帶到不同地方重新對焦。
