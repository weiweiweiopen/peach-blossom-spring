_______________MATERIAL_ADVENTURES_______________

………………the boardgame adventure for humans and other materials

LEVEL2_ THE REMAKE

you set off on a journey with another material you wish to make with .

the goal is to return with an understanding that you are equally remade .

As etextile practitioners we engage with other conductive materials on a regular basis and experience the world of technology as a malleable place. But, in our experience as makers, how much do we experience our own making? This workshop is a game we will play together with other materials in order to sensitize ourselves to our own making.

explore this page in  authren to find out more about how to join us in playing the game >>  https://authren.games/?remake
