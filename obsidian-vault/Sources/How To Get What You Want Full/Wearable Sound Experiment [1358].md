## Content

Produced as an example for a workshop, this sleeve combines a range of fabric components.

Including:

– Neoprene triple AA battery pouch

– Neoprene speaker pouch

– Neoprene Bend Sensor

– Sound toy circuit with poppers attached

– LilyPad Arduino

– Isolated stretchy conductive fabric traces

– Poppers as connectors
