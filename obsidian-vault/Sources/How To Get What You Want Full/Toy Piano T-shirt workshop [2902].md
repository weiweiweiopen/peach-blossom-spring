Location: The Swedish School of Textiles, Boras Sweden

Date: 8/12 Wednesday 10.30-16.30

9/12 Thursday 09.00-16.30

10/12 Friday 09.00-16.30

This workshop is for Textile Design 3rd year students to have a hands on experience on smart textiles and get familiar with basic electronics. We will modify a toy piano into a wearable instrument in this 3 days workshop. Students are encouraged to experiment with various fabric buttons and switches and come up with their own idea and design. No previous knowledge on electronics is required.
