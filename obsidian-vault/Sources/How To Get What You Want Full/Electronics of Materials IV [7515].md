# Electronics of Materials IV

## Content

February 11-15 2019, taught by  Paula Te and Hannah Perner-Wilson at the  Copenhagen Institute for Interaction Design in Copenhagen, Denmark

How might we imagine and build electronics differently, if instead of thinking about electronic functionality in terms of discrete components, we learn to control the flow of electricity through different materials? In this introductory course, electronics are presented as materials or rather, materials and craft techniques are presented for their ability to shape how we interact with electricity.

We hope not only to explain the basic principles and properties of electronics in understandable ways, but also to inspire a craft approach to building electronic components yourself so that you can invent new kinds of interactions.
