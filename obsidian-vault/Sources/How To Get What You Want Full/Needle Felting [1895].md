Needle felting seems to be a great technique for constructing sensors from conductive, resistive materials mashed up with regular felting wool.

Tools for needle felting include:

– Felting needles

– Felting brush

First try at needle felting a pressure sensor from aluminium fibers from  www.softexpressions.com and felting wool from  Clover.
