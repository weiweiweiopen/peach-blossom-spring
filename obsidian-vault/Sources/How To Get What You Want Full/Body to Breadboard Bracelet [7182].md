## Content

Inspired by this upcoming workshop during  E-Textile Spring Break titled “ Body to Body: RFID + PAN Workshop, where Ingo + Nicole will “…introduce a DIY implementation of these systems to pass data through bodies to other bodies”. To make the connection between the body and the breadboard, this bracelet has conductive fabric on the back/bottom side of the bracelet which is connected to a jumper wire that can be inserted to the breadboard.
