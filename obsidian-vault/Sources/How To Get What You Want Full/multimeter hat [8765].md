## Content

this multimeter hat allows me to visualize the conductivity of a material i’m holding in my hands. the only problem is that i can’t see the visualization myself since it happens ontop of my head. thus i rely on the faces of the people looking at me, interpreting their reactions.

the tip of this hat indicates electrical conductivity by lighting up more LEDs. the more LEDs light up, the more conductive the material in my hands is. the less LEDs light up, the more resistance there is in the material i’m holding in my hands. bellow a sketch of the circuit:
