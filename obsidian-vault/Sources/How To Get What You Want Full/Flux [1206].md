## Content

Flux is “a chemical cleaning agent which facilitates soldering, brazing, and welding by removing oxidation from the metals to be joined.” and additionally, it “allows solder to flow easily on the working piece rather than forming beads as it would otherwise.” (wikipedia) So, my problem of solder getting crumbly and not dropping onto the fabric may improve by using flux.
