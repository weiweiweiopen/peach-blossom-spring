## Content

Want to build a full-body motion-capture system, but lazy to sew a human-size suit? This tutorial shows how to make a motion-capture costume for action figures. Flexpoint bend sensors are placed on the joints to capture their movement and position. An  x-OSC is used to capture the analog sensor data and send it via WiFi to a nearby computer. A Processing sketch graphs the sensor data.
