“ohne mich” (engl. “without me”)

Interactive Fiber-Optic Poetry in Motion

For the opening collection of our  KOBA Maßschneiderei – an E-Textile Tailor Shop we are opening in Berlin very soon – we are prototyping the items in this collection. One of these is fiber-optic poetry, two words embroidered in fiber-optics cables, but overlaid so that you only see them when they light up, one after the other.

Flickr set:  https://www.flickr.com/photos/plusea/albums/72157690450178046

 

With only one word embroidered, you can already read it when it is not light up.

 

 

The overlay obscures the individual words, and the light reveals them.

 

For a first prototype I used Maurin’s Lulu module which is a bright SMD LED mounted infront of a tube socket that can then receive the ends of the fiber optic cables.

lulu:  http://etextile-summercamp.org/swatch-exchange/lulu/

fiber optics:  http://wiki.datapaulette.org/doku.php/atelier/documentation/materiautheque/materiaux/electronique_textile/actionneurs_textiles/fibres_optiques
