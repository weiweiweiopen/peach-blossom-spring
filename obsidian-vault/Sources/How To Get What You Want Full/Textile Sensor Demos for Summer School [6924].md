## Content

These textile sensor demos were made for the  Soft Circuits for Soft Bodies workshop at the eTextile Summer School 2017.

Demo Sensors:

– Bend sensor on felt

– Bend sensor on stretch fake leather

– Pressure sensors on felt insole (Sonic Insoles)

– Analog tilt potentiometer on felt

– Squeeze sensor

Arduino sketch and Max/msp patch used during the course
