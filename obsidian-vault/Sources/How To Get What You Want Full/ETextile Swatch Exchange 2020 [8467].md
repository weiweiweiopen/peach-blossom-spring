What have you been up to this past year?

A wide open call for samples of your etextile craft

_deadline: January 7 2021_

At the end of a year in which the diverse measures taken to live with the covid virus have changed the ways in which we are able to be together with other humans, many of us continued to spend time with the materials, tools and techniques of electronic textiles.

The 2020 ETextile Swatch Exchange calls for us to share how we’ve spent the past year of our practice, by exchanging physical samples of our craft in the form of swatches.

link to wide open call:  http://etextile-summercamp.org/swatch-exchange/2020-call/
