## Content

Three techniques for mounting LEDS on top of buttons.

1

sew buttons with a solderable conductive thread and solder LEDs afterwards to the thread.

2

prepare button with copper tape and solder LED first and then sew to make contact to the copper tape pads.

3

same as 2 but mount LED underneath button facing upwards!
