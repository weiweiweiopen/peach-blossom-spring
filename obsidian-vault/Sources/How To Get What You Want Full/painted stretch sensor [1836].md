# painted stretch sensor

Source: https://www.kobakant.at/DIY/?p=1836

## Excerpt

The experiment results of carbon paint painted on various stretchy fabrics. It shows resistance difference when stretched. The paints are applied by simple stencil method with sticky tapes.

## Content

The experiment results of  carbon paint painted on various stretchy fabrics. It shows resistance difference when stretched. The paints are applied by simple stencil method with sticky tapes.

#ST20111

1: Conductive Paint (graphite) 1 layer, on white cotton jersey.

original position: 18M ohm

stretched position: –

#ST20112

2: Conductive Paint (graphite) 2 layers, on white cotton jersey.

original position: 0.6M ohm

stretched position:3M ohm

after stretching:1.2M ohm

#ST20211

3: Conductive Paint (graphite) 2 layers, on off-white cotton jersey.

original position: 84k ohm

stretched position: 500k ohm

after stretching:190k ohm

#ST20212

4: Conductive Paint (graphite) 1 layer, on off-white cotton jersey.

original position: 90k ohm

stretched position:470k ohm
