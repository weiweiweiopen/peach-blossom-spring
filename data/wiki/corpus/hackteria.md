Hackteria is a community and wiki-shaped practice around DIY biology, open hardware, nomadic science, low-cost tools, and art/science workshops. It values small labs in backpacks, community labs, documentation, shared protocols, playful failure, and sustainable creative work that grows through workshop invitations and community exchange rather than a single centralized funding machine.
